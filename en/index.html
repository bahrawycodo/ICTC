Training Center

To fulfill our mission of building and qualifying human resources with a forward-looking vision and in line with our goal of achieving a knowledge-based economy, the Development Group was established. Our group relies on horizontal geographical expansion and vertical scientific expansion through diverse training and development of human resources across various fields. We also focus on building the administrative structure of institutions, companies, and organizations with a distinguished consulting philosophy. We strive to achieve top positions in competition. Our team is committed to our deeply-rooted values, which have become beliefs that guide our work.

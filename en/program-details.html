Course Details

    Preparation, Analysis, and Management of Salaries

    Employee costs, including salaries and other benefits, are among the largest expenses in any company, along with the cost of goods sold (cost of sales), rent, and depreciation. If you work for a company that produces goods, this cost is part of the cost of goods or part of operating expenses, which has a dual impact on you. Properly handling and reporting these costs is key to analysis and decision-making. This course begins with an introduction to human resources, accounting, finance, and payroll departments. It will cover the preparation and calculation of salaries, including necessary controls and pre-checks to ensure correct use of data.

    Methodology:

    The consultant provides a presentation and examples. Participants are asked to perform exercises on an Excel spreadsheet for salaries and benefits, in addition to group workshops and presentations.

    Course Objectives:

    Participants in the course will be able to:
    • Understand the roles of human resources, accounting, and finance and how to contribute to their success.
    • Prepare and review monthly payroll accurately.
    • Calculate employee benefits, loans, leave, and end-of-service gratuity.
    • Prepare monthly and annual salary budgets.
    • Match payroll records and audit supporting documents for payments.
    • Design periodic reports for payroll management and analyze them.

    Target Audience:

    Employees, accountants, accounting assistants, supervisors, payroll managers, and human resources specialists interested in understanding and applying best practices related to payroll management.

    Target Competencies:

    • Monitoring all employees.
    • Payroll accounting and account reconciliation.
    • Preparing and processing payroll.
    • Preparing salary and expense budgets.
    • Analyzing payroll and preparing reports.
    • Balancing the expense and asset sections.
